sylva by escalant3

A relaxed-scheme graph database

A suite for visualization and analysis of graph data Graphs are mathematical concepts with a group of properties that allow a deep analysis of the links between the different objects of the studied environment. Sylva aims to manage complexity in a straightforward manner and allows the researcher to share the results with other colleagues.
